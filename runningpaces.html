• Easy/Conversational Pace (Effort 3/10): You can hold a full conversation; feels relaxed and sustainable for long distances. Essential for building your aerobic base and recovery.
  • Tempo/Threshold Pace (Effort 7/10): "Comfortably hard," just below lactate threshold; you can speak short sentences but not chat easily. Builds stamina.
  • Interval/Fartlek Pace (Effort 9/10): Fast, hard efforts (minutes long) with recovery jogs in between. Improves VO2 max and top speed.
  • Recovery Pace (Effort 1-2/10): Slower than easy pace, used after hard workouts to flush out muscles.
  • Sprint/All-Out (Effort 10/10): Your fastest possible speed for very short distances (e.g., 100m).
