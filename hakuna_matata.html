everything you need to know about running

different types

track

  • running/sprinting in a circle (400m) around a track
  • race distance: generally range from 100m to 5000m (5km)

    • cross country

    • usually on trail, flat not guaranteed (can have torturous hill(s))
    • race distance: generally always around 5km

    trail

    • on trails
    • elevation almost guaranteed
    • race distance: any distance, ranging from vertical kilometer, to ultramarathons (can be hundreds of miles)

    road

    • on paved road
    • flat (0 elevation) not guaranteed
    • race distance: can range from mile to ultramarathons, but generally all famous marathons
