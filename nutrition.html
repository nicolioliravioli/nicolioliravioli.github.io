food guidlines

  • do not eat lots of food and/or protein before a race
  • eat simple easy to digest carbs (try to stick with food you've already tried before)

simple food ideas

good food you can eat before a run, during a run, and after a run

  • peanut butter banana bagel
  • avocado toast
  • candy
  • trail mix
  • pickles
  • watermelon
