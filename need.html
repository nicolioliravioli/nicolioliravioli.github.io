what you need

more specifically what you need to survive, and what you don't need
to survive, hype music, etc.

if lacking in motivation/sad
  1. hype music (especially when need motivation)
  2. candy
  3. friends, if you have them, and they don't hate running
if warm day
  1. water, either in running vest or in hand held water bottle, or every
    few miles on running path
  2. something salty, either salt tablets or salty candy or food, or electrolytes in water
  3. sun hat
  4. put on sunscreen, you don't want skin cancer (as a matter of fact, put on sunscreen even if it isn't super warm and sunny)
  5. maybe a friend or two to make the suffering better
if so cold your body won't warm up in normal running clothes
  1. gloves
  2. long sleeved t-shirt
  3. arm sleeves
  4. warm hat/earwarmers
other optional fun things
  • body glitter
  • body paint
  • dogs
  • that one friend that makes everything better
